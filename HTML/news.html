Indian Stars News Bulletin - April 2, 2016

Historically we have only allowed one account in Indian Stars... In other words no mini accounts were allowed. Senior leadership has wrestled with this question and decided that you can now bring your second (mini) account to Indian Stars only if you are a long standing member of Indian Stars and with co-leader approval. Why? Simply if you havent proven yourself as a loyal member and decide to leave Indian Stars we lose 2 accounts which is extremely painful if war is underway.
