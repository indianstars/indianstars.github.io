LEADERSHIP

The leadership of Indian Stars is divided across our clans. The leaders are responsible for the protection and smooth operation of the clan and each leader represents the clan in a unique way. The leadership for Indian Stars were handpicked based on the following criteria:

  • Follow and enforce the rules
  • Active within the clan
  • Help and encourage others
  • Provide advice
  • Excellent donator
  • Loyalty

Further, to help spread the work across the clan and provide members a chance to lead within the clan we select interim elders to help manage the clan. The interim elders are selected as needed by the clan leadership. Interim elders are selected for a 4-6 week (2-3 season rotation). At the end of the rotation, new interim elders are selected to help the clan grow. Members that have served as Interim elder can be selected again for a future rotation.

Many members of Indian Stars are elder material but not everyone can be a clan leader. By setting the elders at a fixed number and implementing a rotating elder position there is no competition within the clan. Further the elders know each other well and work seamlessly together on helping the clan function smoothly. The clan can focus on helping each other grow stronger and form strong relational bonds.
